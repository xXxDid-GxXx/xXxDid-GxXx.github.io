Name of project

Carbon Focus

Key details

Role: UI/UX Designer

Goal: Design a landing page that communicates the value of a sustainability platform

Tools: Figma, GPT

Timeline: 3 weeks

Project summary

Concept landing page for a digital sustainability service. The goal was to design a trust-building interface that translates a complex environmental solution into an engaging web experience through clarity and visual storytelling.

Visual language

The visual direction is built around nature aesthetics. Sweeping landscapes, soft gradients, and curved dividers create a calm, organic feel. Green tones reinforce the environmental focus, while high-contrast CTAs maintain engagement.

Explaining complexity

Clarity: Direct, scannable sections with headers and examples.

Actionability: Every finding ties to a concrete solution.

Visual Appeal: Infographics and annotated screenshots break up text.

Emphasizing Tangible Benefits

Rather than listing features, I highlighted direct outcomes: environmental impact, inventory management, and compliance. Each benefit is paired with real-world imagery for a memorable value proposition.

Trust Through Testimonials and Partners

Testimonial quotes and partner logos build credibility. The section is minimal and balanced, letting social proof speak without overwhelming the layout.

Smooth Scroll to Action

The page ends with FAQ content and a contact form. Collapsible FAQs reduce cognitive load while addressing concerns. The final CTA is clean and focused.
